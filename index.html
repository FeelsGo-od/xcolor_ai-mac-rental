About XColor

A global marketplace for compute power

XColor is a distributed compute network that connects people who need GPU power with those who have it. Instead of relying on centralized data centers, we enable a global marketplace of machines — from efficient Mac minis to high-performance GPUs.

Our platform allows developers, researchers, and companies to run workloads on demand, while hardware owners can monetize their idle resources. By leveraging global differences in electricity and hardware costs, we make compute significantly more affordable.

XColor is built for flexibility: run AI models, batch jobs, rendering tasks, or automation workflows — all without managing infrastructure.

Our Mission

To make compute power accessible, affordable, and decentralized — unlocking a global network where anyone can use or provide GPU resources.
